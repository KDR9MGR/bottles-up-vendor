Delete Account

We are sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your Bottles Up Vendor account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete via App (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to delete your data immediately.

  1. Open the Bottles Up Vendor App
  2. Go to Profile > Security Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your choice

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email. We will process your request within 7 days.

Please click the button below to send us an email request. Make sure to send it from the email address associated with your account.

Request Account Deletion

Data Safety Information:
Deleting your account is permanent. All your data including profile information, event history, and bookings will be permanently removed from our servers in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
